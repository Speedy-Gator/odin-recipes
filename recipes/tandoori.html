Tandoori Chicken Recipe

Description

This chicken is off the charts!. The recipe comes from India, it is filled with different flavors, and the best part, very easy to make in a time crunch!.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup of plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons of ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon of ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 inch piece of fresh ginger, peeled & grated
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the yogurt, lemon juice, cumin, paprika, turmeric, coriander, cayenne pepper, garlic, ginger, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the bowl and coat them thoroughly with the marinade. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours overnight.
  3. Preheat the oven to 400 F. Place the chicken pieces on a baking sheet and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the skin is crispy.
  4. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve with naan bread, rice, or salad.
